Hostel Guidelines

  1. Check-In and Check-Out:
    Check-In: Check-in time is from 7:00 am. Early check-in is possible based on availability.
    Check-Out: Check-out time is 11:00 pm. Late check-out requests can be accommodated for an additional fee.
  2. Quiet Hours:
    Please observe quiet hours from 6:00 am to 11:00 am to ensure a peaceful atmosphere for all guests.
  3. Security:
    Your safety is our priority. We have 24/7 security in place. Lockers are available for your valuables; please use them.
  4. Room Rules:
    Shared dormitories are designed for comfort and shared living. Please keep your area tidy.
    Be considerate of fellow guests' privacy and maintain appropriate behavior.
    Lockers are available; please store your belongings securely.
  5. Common Areas:
    Our common areas are shared spaces for relaxation and socializing. Please clean up after yourself to keep these areas enjoyable for all guests.
  6. Kitchen Use:
    If you use the communal kitchen, ensure it remains clean and organized.
    Label your food, and be considerate of others sharing the space.
  7. Respect for Others:
    Treat fellow guests with respect and courtesy, regardless of their background or beliefs.
  8. Guests and Visitors:
    Visitors are allowed in common areas but not permitted in dormitories.
    The maximum occupancy for each room is [Max Occupancy].
  9. Social Events:
    Join our social events and activities to make the most of your stay. Details can be found on our notice board or website.
  10. Complaint Resolution:
    If you have any concerns or complaints, please inform the front desk or email us at ietdavv@gmail.com.
  11. Check-Out Procedures:
    Check-out involves returning keys, settling any outstanding bills, and ensuring the room is left in good condition.
    Luggage storage is available after check-out.
  12. Lost and Found:
    If you lose an item during your stay, please report it to the front desk.
    Found items will be kept for Lost and Found Retention Period.
  13. Payment and Fees:
    Payment methods accepted are [Accepted Payment Methods].
    Additional fees may apply for services like laundry or late check-out.
    A security deposit of 5000 may be required upon check-in.
  14. Housekeeping:
    Housekeeping services are provided once per day.
    Guests are responsible for maintaining the cleanliness of their living spaces.
  15. Environmental Responsibility:
    We encourage eco-friendly practices such as conserving energy and water, recycling, and reducing waste.
  16. Emergency Procedures:
    In case of fire or other emergencies, follow the posted evacuation routes and contact 123456789.
    A first-aid kit is available at the front desk.
  17. Additional Services:
    Additional services such as laundry facilities, tours, and transportation assistance are available. Inquire at the front desk for more information.
  18. Feedback and Reviews:
    Your feedback is valuable to us. Please leave a review on our website to help us improve our services.
  19. Contact Information:
    For any questions or assistance, you can reach our staff at 1256789755.

Mess Guidelines for IET DAVV

Section 1: Introduction

Welcome to IET DAVV Dining. We are pleased to offer a range of dining facilities and services designed to meet your needs.

Section 2: Meal Schedule

Our meal schedule is as follows:

  • Breakfast: 8:00 am
  • Lunch: 11:00 am
  • Dinner: 7:00 pm

Please note that special meal schedules may apply on weekends or holidays.

Section 3: Meal Plans

Breakfast:

  • Scrambled eggs
  • Toast with butter and jam
  • Oatmeal with honey and fruits
  • Fresh fruit salad
  • Milk, tea, or coffee

Lunch:

  • Grilled chicken or tofu (for vegetarians/vegans)
  • Steamed brown rice or quinoa
  • Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, and green beans)
  • Salad with a variety of fresh greens and dressing
  • Lentil soup (optional)
  • Fruit yogurt (or dairy-free alternative)
  • Water or flavored water

Snack:

  • Mixed nuts
  • Fresh fruit (e.g., apples, bananas)
  • Hummus with carrot and cucumber sticks
  • Cheese and crackers (optional)
  • Herbal tea or water
